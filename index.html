Interviewing Kyla Maddalena

I got together with Kyla Maddalena and asked her a few questions that I felt would be interesting to know about her. Here are the results to that endevor.

November 12, 2019

Who Has Influenced You The Most?

No one really. I just do my own thing.

What Has Been Your Best Moment This Year (So Far)?

I got engaged to my life-long best friend and love of my life over the summer.

What Would You Like To Change About Yourself?

Ideally, I’d like to lose weight, but I’m not very motivated to do so. My fiancee says she likes me the way I am so I guess I won't be doing anything about it.

What Motivates You To Get Up Every Morning?

I get to see my cat, who I love very much, and then I get to leave and see my friends or my fiancee.

If You Could Do Anything For The Rest Of Your Life, What Would That Be?

I would like to draw or spend time with the family I’m going to have. My fiancee and I are going to have at least one kid. If we have a daughter we’re going to name her Evelyn.

If You Could Live Anywhere In The World For A Year, Where Would It Be?

Vancouver. It’s about as far away from here as I could get without leaving Canada.

What Would You Like To Eat For Your Last Meal?

Sit me in an all-you-can-eat buffet and then my “last meal” would be endless.

Would You Rather Be Bald Or Covered From Head To Toe In Hair?

Bald. It would be nice never having to shave again. Just give me a wig and I’m good.

What aspects of graphic design to you gravitate towards?

I like both digital and physical media. I dropped 2k on a laptop/tablet just for artistic purposes

Why do you want to be a designer?

It’s the only career choice that highschool made me somewhat passionate about. I was in the Yearbook class at Notre Dame grades 10-12 and it was the only class I ever got an average of 100 in.
